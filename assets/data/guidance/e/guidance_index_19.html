Guidance for increased environmental sustainability

P7. Enabling smallholder exits from the livestock sector may reduce pressure on land and water resources and provide more stable livelihoods that are more resilient to climate change and weather variability.
P7. Livelihood diversification will enable producers to subsist on alternative sources of income during shocks, increasing their resilience to climate change and weather variability.
