Activities commonly implemented under this intervention

Provide extension agents with training and capacity building.
Develop extension manuals and curricula.

Guidance for increased environmental sustainability

P2. P7. Train extension agents to evaluate and advise producers on sustainable grassland management and sustainable use of water resources.
P7. Train extension agents to collect grassland degradation data and other environmental indicators, such as biodiversity.
P7. Embed environmental management practices in basic extension work and extension manuals.
